Privacy Policy for In The Zone

In The Zone is designed to help users take notes while watching YouTube videos. We prioritize user privacy and data security.

Data Collection

When using In The Zone:

  • Notes, timestamps, and preferences are stored locally using Chrome’s storage system.
  • If you choose to sign in, your data may also be securely stored in our database (via Supabase) to enable syncing across devices.

When signing in with Google:

  • We receive basic account information such as your email address and a unique user ID.
  • We do not access your Google Drive, contacts, or any other personal data.
  • We only use your Google account information for authentication and do not access or store any additional Google user data.

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Save and sync your notes
  • Associate your data with your account
  • Improve the functionality of the extension

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising

Data Storage

  • Local data is stored using Chrome’s storage system.
  • Cloud data is stored securely using Supabase.

User Control

  • You can log out at any time to stop cloud syncing.
  • Your locally stored data will remain on your device unless manually deleted.

Third-Party Services

  • We use Supabase for authentication and data storage.
  • We use Google OAuth for secure login.
